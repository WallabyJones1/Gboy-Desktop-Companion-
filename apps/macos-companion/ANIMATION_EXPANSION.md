# Lore-Informed Animation Expansion

This file turns the current Neuko / G*Boy lore into a much larger animation and behavior backlog for the companion app.

## Tone Targets

- Escaped subject, not mascot.
- Smart, alert, uneasy, occasionally funny.
- Memetic warfare over random chaos.
- Anti-Harmony, anti-control, pro-signal, pro-escape.
- Feels like he is seeding warnings, symbols, and traces for operatives.

## Source Anchors

- G*Boy is an escaped Saint Juniper subject and symbolic resistance figure.
- The Bloc frames him through memetic rebellion, symbolism, stickers, graffiti, and hijacked broadcasts.
- Harmony is associated with sedation, homogeny, and control.
- The world mixes surveillance, occult ritual, psychic experiments, and the Mindverse.
- BLOWPIE gives him a nervous companion dynamic.

## Smart Behavior Rules

- He should not behave randomly all the time.
- He should cluster activities by mood.
- Hungry Gboy does domestic or scavenging loops.
- Alert Gboy does surveillance, radio, note leaving, and edge peeking.
- Rebellious Gboy does graffiti, stickers, manifesto drops, and hijacks.
- Tired Gboy makes nests, watches CRT static, or sleeps lightly.
- High-trust or happy Gboy plays games, cooks, dances, or chats with BLOWPIE.
- Rare high-strangeness states trigger portal work, artifact inspection, and Mindverse scenes.

## Animation Production Strategy

- Start with prop-light loops first: marker, stickers, TV, handheld, radio, terminal, fries, noodles.
- Next build medium props: couch, CRT, skillet, phone, evidence wall.
- Save the expensive set-pieces for later: portals, flying ship, throne, full graffiti spread, newsroom desk.
- Treat slogan-writing and sticker-slapping as modular systems so multiple phrases can reuse the same body animation.
